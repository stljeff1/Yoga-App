#PropifyYourLife

I am a student of Iyengar Yoga and web development. This website is an ongoing project, serving three functions:

  1. To give myself an opportunuity to develop a fully functioning web application from scratch using the AngularJS Framework.
  2. To systemtize my own learning of the asanas (poses) and the subject of yoga.
  3. To attempt document the multitude of ways of performing an asana, as well as all the effects they produce on the individual

I am heavily influenced by Iyengar Yoga because I have benefitted greatly from the method's systematic approach to learning yoga. Iyengar yoga is about blah blah blah.

Aside from my technical reasons to build this site, I have started this project to further my own understanding of yoga. This site is not meant to be anything more than that.
